The Life and Existence of one
Ryan McFarlane

It began on a dreary September day that I was born. It was the beginning of the world's greatest protagoni- oh my bad I meant PROCRASTINATOR.
As a Web/Interactive Media my speciality lies in making websites and making them look nice. (The only way to explain my major to my family that they understand...seriously). Even though in the end, I am simply telling something with the intelligence of a rock what to do.
I happen to have been born with colorblindness and ADHD, two things that can make my daily life and education in design difficult. Though, ADHD can actually be helpful with how it has changed how my brain functions. I process things quickly, and learn things even faster. I am a Jack of all Trades, and yet a master of none.
Coding wasn't something I expected to enjoy so much but it made sense. I come from a family of artists with my mom being a graphite artist and my sister a photography. They can always make some amazing visual art but I never could...but I could write. Learning to write code and seeing what I write become something visually is the most amazing feeling in the world. As a writer, code was just another way to write, and front end work ended up feeling natural.
Oh, I also like gaming, writing, anime, manga, learning new things, and I am an expert on random facts that aren't helpful but make you scratch your head and go "Really? Huh I never would've thought that".
